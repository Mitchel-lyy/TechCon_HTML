History

The name "Techcon" is associated with a few different entities, primarily in the fields of post-tensioning, fluid dispensing, and technology conferences. Techcon Systems, Inc., founded in 1985, is a post-tensioning specialist, while Techcon Systems, an OK International product group, has a long history in fluid dispensing systems, celebrating 55 years in 2016

Mission

The mission of TechCon, regardless of the specific TechCon, generally revolves around driving technological innovation, connecting industry leaders, and fostering growth within the tech ecosystem. This often involves organizing events, providing resources, and facilitating collaboration between entrepreneurs, investors, and other key players in the tech industry.
